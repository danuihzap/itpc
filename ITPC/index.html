Welcome to ITPC Sydney Website

Indonesian Trade Promotion Centre, or known as ITPC is a government organisation under coordination of Ministry of Trade of the Republic of Indonesia, with a common goal to enhance the export of Indonesian products throughout the world.
ITPC Sydney provides services for the Australian business partners with facilitation to enhance two-way trade engagements.
